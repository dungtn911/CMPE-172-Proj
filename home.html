Forever Friends

Working together to save dogs.

Our Mission

This is a project for our Enterprise Software class. For this project, we decided to make a mock database that can help dog shelters.

Almost 3.3 million dogs enter dog shelters every year. Because of this, some dog shelters struggle with housing dogs and getting them adopted quickly to do so. These shelters cannot make this process faster and easier if there are not enough resources. Sometimes, they lack the proper organization and structure to determine which dogs need to be adopted or which factors contribute to this process.
